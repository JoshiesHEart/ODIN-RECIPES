Jollof Rice Recipe

Description

A vibrant heap of smoky jollof rice,perfectly seasonedand stained a deep sunset orangefrom a rich tomato and pepper base. Nestled alonside are tender, charred chunks of seasoned beef, offering a savory, protein-packed contrast to the spicy, aromatic grains

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or turkey stock
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Chicken or beef (optional)

Preparations

  1. Blend tomatoes, pepper and onions together
  2. Fry the toamto paste in oil for a few minutes
  3. Add the blended mixture and cook until the water dries a bit
  4. Add seasonings cubes, curry, thyme, and salt
  5. Pour in the stock and allow it to boil
  6. Add washed rice and stir well
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice becomes soft
  8. Serve with fried plantain, chicken, or salad
