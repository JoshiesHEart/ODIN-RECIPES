Afang-soup Recipe

Description

Afang Soup is a and flavorful Nigerian delicacy made with fresh afang leaves ,waterleaf,assorted meats,fish, and traditional seasonings. Known for its deep taste and nourishing ingredients, this soup is a perfect blend of aroma, spice, and texture

Popular in southern Nigeria, especially among the Efik and Ibibio people, Afang soup is best enjoyed with swallow foods like pounded yam, eba, or fufu. Every spoonful delivers a delicious homemade taste that brings comfort and satisfaction

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced afang leaves
  • 3 cups waterleaf
  • Assorted meat
  • Dry or smoked fish
  • Periwinkle (optional)
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning Cubes
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Water or meat stock

Preparations

  1. Wash and season the assorted meat, then boil until soft
  2. Add stock fish and dry fish, then allow everything to cook together
  3. Pour in palm oil,pepper,crayfish,onions and seasoning cubes
  4. Add chopped waterleaf and cook for a few minute
  5. Stir in the afang leaves and mix properly
  6. Allow to simmer for about 5 minutes until the leaves soften and the soup thickens slightly
  7. Add salt if needed and stir well

Serve hot with pounded yam, fufu, eba, or semovita
